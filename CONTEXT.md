# HF Software Landing Site

A marketing site for HF Software, a software agency. Its job is to showcase delivered work and convert visitors into leads via a contact form.

## Language

**Home Page**:
The primary entry point — hero, case study previews, and the contact form.
_Avoid_: Landing page (when referring to the whole site), main page

**Case Study**:
A dedicated page that tells the story of one delivered client project: the problem, what was built, and the outcome.
_Avoid_: Project page, portfolio item, work sample

**Case Study Structure**:
Three sections — The Problem, What We Built, The Result — kept short enough to read on mobile in under ninety seconds.
_Avoid_: Full portfolio write-up, technical deep-dive, phased timeline

**Agroshow Narrative**:
Tell the project's evolution as one coherent story in a single paragraph — platform rebuild, operational transformation, assistant as a final beat — not as numbered phases.
_Avoid_: Project timeline, milestone list, phase breakdown

**Case Study Assets**:
Screenshots copied from the portfolio project, optimized and stored in-repo — not placeholders or hotlinked live-site captures.
_Avoid_: Stock imagery, live embeds, external image URLs

**Case Study Order**:
Agroshow appears first on the home page, then Terepaima — leading with the platform whose operational side transformed most dramatically.
_Avoid_: Chronological order, alphabetical order

**Get in Touch**:
The primary conversion action — submitting the contact form to start a conversation with HF Software.
_Avoid_: Contact us, reach out, book a call

**Case Study CTA**:
A closing prompt at the bottom of each case study page — a feeling-first nudge toward the contact form, not a duplicated form.
_Avoid_: Inline form, hard sell, secondary hero

**Lead**:
A small or mid-size business operator (owner, manager, or decision-maker) who visits the site and may submit the contact form.
_Avoid_: Prospect, client, user

**Operational Software**:
Custom web platforms that replace legacy tools and support a business's day-to-day workflows.
_Avoid_: Digital solutions, software solutions, tech stack

**Copy Tone**:
Sell a feeling — relief, trust, humanity — not a product or feature list. Short, intentional text; no hollow technical jargon.
_Avoid_: Feature selling, buzzwords, solution-oriented copy

**AI Assistant**:
A capability within a delivered project — mentioned when relevant, never the headline or selling point.
_Avoid_: AI-powered, AI-first, intelligent solutions, RAG

**Visual Tone**:
Warm, paper-like, with restrained hand-drawn accents — intentional imperfection that feels human-made, not polished corporate or generic AI aesthetic.
_Avoid_: Sleek, futuristic, glassmorphic

**Accent Color**:
Blue `#2563EB` (Tailwind blue-600) — used for CTAs, links, sketch strokes, and the monogram. Single accent; no gradients or secondary brand colors in v1.
_Avoid_: Brand palette, primary color (when listing multiple colors)

**Motion**:
Quiet, organic animation — sketch lines that draw in, gentle fade-ups on scroll, subtle interactions. The site should feel like it's breathing, not performing.
_Avoid_: Bouncy effects, parallax, demo-reel transitions

**Typography**:
Instrument Sans for headings, Alegreya for body text, IBM Plex Mono for metadata and labels.
_Avoid_: Single-font layouts, decorative display fonts

**Agency Name**:
HF Software — a placeholder; the site may be rebranded when the final name is chosen.
_Avoid_: Company name, brand (when referring specifically to the naming decision)

**Wordmark**:
A hand-drawn HF monogram in SVG paired with "Software" in Instrument Sans — human-made, easy to swap when the final name is chosen.
_Avoid_: Logo, brand mark, icon (when referring to the header identity)

**Agency Voice**:
Collective "we" — the site speaks as HF Software, not as individuals. No team section or headshots; work carries the trust.
_Avoid_: Freelancer portfolio framing, founder-led, about us page

**Navigation**:
Sticky header with wordmark, locale toggle, and Get in Touch. No hamburger menu — case studies are discovered via home page cards; footer holds secondary links.
_Avoid_: Main menu, site map, multi-level nav

**Privacy**:
A minimal page explaining that contact-form data is emailed to HF Software and not sold or used for marketing without consent. No analytics or tracking cookies in v1.
_Avoid_: Cookie banner, privacy policy (legal jargon), GDPR page

**Company Stage**:
A contact-form field where the lead describes their operational situation — not business maturity, but the problem they're facing.
_Avoid_: Company size, business stage, maturity level

**Referral Source**:
A contact-form field where the lead indicates how they found HF Software — referral, search, social media, a project, or other.
_Avoid_: How did you find us, acquisition channel, lead source

**Locale**:
A language version of the site — English (`/en/`) or Spanish (`/es/`). English is the default; Spanish uses neutral international wording, not regional dialects.
_Avoid_: Language, translation, i18n (when speaking to visitors)
